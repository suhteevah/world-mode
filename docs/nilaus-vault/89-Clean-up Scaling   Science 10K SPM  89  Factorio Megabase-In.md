## Summary

### Key Lessons
- Yellow science is the final science type to scale to 10K SPM; getting it online completes the full 10K science suite
- A base with zero dashboard warnings means all supply chains are fully saturated; this is the prerequisite for scaling
- Between-session idle time lets the base fill buffers naturally; this is a legitimate strategy for stabilizing before scaling
- Testing and failing is the point of the series, not a detraction from it; uncharted territory requires experimentation

### Design Principles
- Before scaling any science, ensure zero warnings on the dashboard; saturated buffers prevent cascading failures during transition
- Verify buffer levels visually: 24,000+ items in station chests plus full trains waiting = healthy supply
- Let the base run idle between building sessions to naturally fill all buffers before the next scaling push
- Clean up old builds and optimize before adding new capacity; technical debt compounds at megabase scale

### Ratios & Numbers
- Red, blue, green, purple science: all at 10,000 SPM before yellow science scaling
- Blue circuit buffer: 96,000 items idle (64K at stations + 32K in waiting trains)
- Yellow science: scaling from 7,500 to 10,000 SPM in this episode
- Station buffer health indicator: 64,000 items + full train waiting = completely idle/saturated
- Running at 7,500 SPM with stability across all non-yellow sciences confirmed over multiple hours

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not scale to 10K SPM while any dashboard warning is active; fix all supply issues first
- Do not skip the buffer saturation verification; a station showing 64K items + idle train confirms genuine surplus
- Do not rush yellow science scaling; it demands blue circuits, low density structures, and other complex intermediates
- Do not view iteration and reverting as wasted effort; it is how you learn what works at this scale
