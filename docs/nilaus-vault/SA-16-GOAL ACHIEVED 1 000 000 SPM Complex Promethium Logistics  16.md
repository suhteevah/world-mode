## Summary
### Key Lessons
- 1,000,000 SPM achieved for ALL sciences including Promethium; stable for 10+ hours
- Promethium logistics is a complex orchestration problem, not just a production problem
- Biter egg 30-minute expiry drives the entire cycle timing
- 5 ships cycling (4 minimum needed); redundancy ensures stability
- 35 UPS achieved which is considered good for this scale

### Space Age Mechanics
- Promethium science produced ON the collection ships during transit
- Biter eggs loaded at Nauvis, consumed during deep space Promethium collection
- Quantum processors shipped from Aquilo to Promethium ships
- Each ship drops off science in batches of ~2000 four times before departing

### Design Principles
- Everything must sync to 30-minute biter egg expiry cycle
- Ship loading triggers next cycle automatically
- Balance production so no excess and no shortage
- 5 ships provide buffer against timing variations

### Ratios & Numbers
- 1,000,000 science per minute sustained for all 12 sciences
- 5 Promethium ships cycling continuously
- ~220,000 items per ship arrival batch
- 35 UPS at full operation (acceptable performance)
- 10+ hours of verified stable operation with zero maintenance

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not attempt Promethium science without all logistics tested and verified
- 30-minute cycle is sacred; any delay cascades into failures
- Do not reduce ship count below 4; 5 provides necessary safety margin
- UPS will suffer; use particle removal mod and accept reduced game speed
