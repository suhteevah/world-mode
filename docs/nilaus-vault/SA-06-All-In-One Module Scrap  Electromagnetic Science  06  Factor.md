## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Legendary Holmium is not viable for megabase scale; massive infrastructure for tiny output
- All-in-one Fulgora city block: scrap in, electromagnetic science out (including rocket components)
- Fulgora scrap locations with high density will essentially never run out at 5000% research productivity
- Each city block produces 120 electromagnetic science/sec; need ~8 blocks for megabase

### Space Age Mechanics
- Fulgora scrap recycling: one resource (scrap) becomes all needed materials
- Holmium processing: quality approach does not scale for megabase; use normal quality bulk production
- Resource drain at 16% with high productivity means nearly infinite scrap patches

### Design Principles
- Self-contained city block: scrap collection, processing, blue circuits, density structures, rocket fuel, science, and rocket launching
- Scalable by stamping down identical blocks on rich scrap locations
- Minimal external dependencies (only scrap input)

### Ratios & Numbers
- 120 electromagnetic science/sec per city block
- Target: 860-960/sec total (8 city blocks needed)
- Research productivity at 5000% makes scrap patches essentially infinite

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not pursue legendary Holmium for megabase; it does not scale
- Ensure blueprint accounts for Fulgora water tiles (landfill may be needed)
- Do not overbuild; each block barely produces what is needed, which is optimal
