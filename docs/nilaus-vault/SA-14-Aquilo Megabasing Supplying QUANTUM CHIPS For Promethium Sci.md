## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Quantum processors are needed for Promethium science; produced on Aquilo
- Only 1 quantum processor needed per science recipe but supply chain is complex
- 200% productivity means 1 quantum processor = 30 quantum chips
- Aquilo megabase with city blocks is described as the best-looking planetary base

### Space Age Mechanics
- Quantum chips produced in Aquilo cryogenic plants with 200% productivity
- Ships launch from Aquilo with quantum processors to Promethium ships
- 19,200 items per automated launch cycle
- Fusion power cells: only 24 needed per full round trip

### Design Principles
- Automated launch cycles triggered by ship arrival
- Multiple launch locations for parallel supply
- 64 rockets per incoming supply batch
- Coordinate with biter egg logistics (next episode)

### Ratios & Numbers
- 1 quantum processor -> 30 quantum chips (with 200% productivity)
- 990 Promethium science per production batch
- 19,200 quantum processors per automated launch cycle
- 28,000 items per supply ship arrival

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Ensure fusion power cell supply does not bottleneck ship operations
- Coordinate launch timing between Aquilo supply and Promethium ships
- Do not try to supply quantum chips from anywhere other than Aquilo
