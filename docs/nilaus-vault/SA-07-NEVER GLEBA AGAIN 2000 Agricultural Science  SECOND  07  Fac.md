## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Gleba is the worst planet; solve it once with massive overbuilding then never return
- Legendary agricultural science via bioflux upcycling does NOT scale (28/sec from massive infrastructure)
- Quality seeds/agriculture towers do nothing; only way to scale is MORE harvesters
- Normal quality bulk production with stampable city blocks is the correct approach
- Spoilage management is critical; ~82% spoil rate on agricultural science

### Space Age Mechanics
- Bioflux upcycling to legendary is a dead end for megabase scale
- Agricultural science spoils during transport; account for ~80% delivery rate
- Biter eggs hatch on Gleba if bioflux runs out; safety systems essential
- City block harvesting design with stampable modules

### Design Principles
- Design three modules: science production, harvesting, and biter safety
- Stampable city blocks for harvesting: just place more for more output
- Double production to account for spoilage losses in transit

### Ratios & Numbers
- 960 agricultural science/sec per production setup (doubled = 1920/sec)
- ~2 million science/min effective after biolab multipliers
- Even at 80% spoilage delivery rate, still 1.5M science/min
- Biolab productivity at 760% = 8.6x multiplier

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not waste time on legendary bioflux/agricultural science (does not scale)
- Always account for spoilage losses in agricultural science calculations
- Ensure biter egg safety systems are in place on all Gleba builds
- Do not underestimate harvesting infrastructure needed
