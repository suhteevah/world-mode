## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Metallurgic science on Vulcanus is straightforward; mainly needs tungsten and molten metals
- Design process: calculate 240/sec per module, then scale with 4 modules for 960/sec
- Coal, calcite, sulfuric acid, and lava are all abundant/easy on Vulcanus
- Tungsten (600/sec per module) is the main throughput challenge

### Space Age Mechanics
- Vulcanus foundry recipes with high productivity for tungsten plate and carbide
- Lava-based production: unlimited lava supply means metal throughput is the constraint
- Carbon from coal processing feeds into tungsten carbide

### Design Principles
- 240 metallurgic science/sec per city block module; 4 modules = 960/sec
- Pipeline throughput for sulfuric acid must be managed with pumps
- Copy-paste scaling of identical modules

### Ratios & Numbers
- 960 metallurgic science/sec achieved (enough for 1M SPM)
- Per 240/sec module: 33 calcite, 96 coal, 600 tungsten, 2400 sulfuric acid
- Peak consumption can spike to 1.9M SPM temporarily
- 30,000 sulfuric acid/sec from dedicated producers

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not forget carbon (coal) requirements for tungsten carbide
- Pipeline extent matters for sulfuric acid; use pumps to segment long runs
- Gleba biters can attack during idle periods; keep bioflux flowing
