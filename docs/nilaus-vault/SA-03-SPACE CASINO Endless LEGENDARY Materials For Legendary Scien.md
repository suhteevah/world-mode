## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Space casino = spaceship that upcycles asteroid chunks to legendary quality via quality modules in reprocessors
- 1 legendary science pack = 6x normal value; also reduces robot/belt load by 6x
- Ship shape matters: collection rate depends on front surface area and speed
- Upcycling will be nerfed in 2.1 (quality modules removed from crushers) but works now
- Use rotating belts with side-loading to prevent jams; overflow protection is critical

### Space Age Mechanics
- Quality modules in reprocessors gradually increase chunk quality to legendary
- Asteroid chunk types: metallic, carbonic, oxide (ice); ice legendary chunks not used for science
- Ship collects asteroids from front; wider ship = more collection
- Red, green, blue, yellow, and space science can all be made legendary from asteroid chunks
- Purple/military cannot be made in space (need stone, no lava source)

### Design Principles
- Template ship design: upcycling section + science production area + buffer storage
- Clusters of 5 reprocessors with rotational belt feeding
- Side-load merging prevents belt jams (better than splitter merging)
- Multiple ships on same template better than one mega-ship

### Ratios & Numbers
- ~40 legendary chunks per minute per ship (becomes significant with productivity multipliers)
- Ship produces enough for science production area on board
- 10-minute stabilization period to reach steady state

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not merge upcycled output with splitters (causes jams); use side-loading instead
- Ensure overflow path exists for when processing backs up
- Do not forget fuel production on the ship itself
- Calcite is the bottleneck resource on space platforms
