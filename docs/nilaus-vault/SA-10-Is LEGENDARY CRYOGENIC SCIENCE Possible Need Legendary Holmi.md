## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Legendary cryogenic science IS possible via legendary Holmium from Fulgora space casino
- 1 legendary Holmium = 15 legendary lithium (5 lithium x 3 from cryogenic plant productivity)
- Lithium plate smelting adds 50% productivity; total chain is very efficient
- Ship legendary Holmium plates from Fulgora to Aquilo for cryogenic science production
- Need 160 legendary cryogenic science/sec (960/6 due to legendary 6x value)

### Space Age Mechanics
- Cryogenic plant gives 3x output on lithium recipe (200% productivity)
- Legendary ice from space casino ships; legendary Holmium from Fulgora recycling
- Cryogenic science needs: ice (abundant), lithium plate (from Holmium), fluoroketone
- Electric furnace for lithium plates: 50% max productivity

### Design Principles
- Fulgora legendary Holmium production feeds into Aquilo cryogenic science
- Space casino ice ships provide legendary ice
- Logistics ship between Fulgora and Aquilo for Holmium transport

### Ratios & Numbers
- 1 legendary Holmium plate -> 15 legendary lithium -> 22.5 legendary lithium plates
- 160 legendary cryogenic science/sec target
- 94,000 Holmium plates stockpiled from established Fulgora production
- 94/sec production per Fulgora cryogenic plant with 6000% productivity

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not try to make legendary Holmium on Aquilo; produce it on Fulgora and ship it
- Stack inserter output limits can cause box overflow; monitor carefully
- Ensure logistics ship between Fulgora and Aquilo runs consistently
