### Space Age Mechanics
- Yellow science requires LDS (low density structures), which are complex to produce in space
- Output bottleneck: LDS production rate limits yellow science throughput
- Research productivity going from 870% to 880% is only ~1% actual increase (diminishing returns)
- Each productivity research costs ~290 million science points at high levels

### Design Principles
- Belt output optimization is critical for LDS; a single misplaced belt turn can bottleneck everything
- Productivity modules in assemblers are essential for space-based production
- Ship must reach steady state before production rate is reliable

### Ratios & Numbers
- Research productivity at 880% achieved
- ~290 million science points per research level at high tiers
- 10% productivity per research = smaller and smaller relative gains

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Watch for output belt bottlenecks on LDS production
