## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Legendary blue science in space is possible but not the most efficient approach
- Red circuits are the bottleneck: 45/sec legendary red circuits needed for 60 blue science/sec
- Legendary plastic requires legendary coal from carbonic asteroid chunks
- Ship route optimization matters: asteroid density varies between planetary routes

### Space Age Mechanics
- Legendary plastic production benefits from ~300% productivity in space
- Asteroid density varies by route: metallic peaks near Vulcanus, carbonic near Gleba
- Total asteroid collection rate ~55-57 chunks across all types on optimal routes
- Blue science needs iron, copper, coal (for plastic), and red circuits

### Design Principles
- Design at 60 legendary science/sec scale even if ship cannot fully sustain it
- Use multiple ships to compensate for production rate limitations
- Route ships between planets for maximum asteroid collection diversity

### Ratios & Numbers
- 60 legendary blue science/sec target per ship
- ~0.9 metallic asteroid chunks/sec consumption rate
- Route asteroid densities: 31/16/8 (metallic/carbonic/oxide) vs 25/25/7 on alternate routes
- Total ~55 chunks/min on either route

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not underestimate red circuit production chain complexity in space
- Route selection matters significantly for asteroid chunk ratios
- Legendary coal supply is the hidden bottleneck for blue science
