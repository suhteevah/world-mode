## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Aquilo needs complete overhaul from old substation grid to city block system
- Two main Aquilo productions: legendary cryogenic science + quantum chips for Promethium
- Ice platforms with 6000% productivity and 16% resource drain are effectively infinite
- City block approach enables modular scaling of all Aquilo production

### Space Age Mechanics
- Aquilo cryogenic plants produce at 94/sec with massive productivity bonuses
- Fluoroketone recycling is essential for sustainable production
- Fusion power cells for spaceships use only 24 per full round trip
- Quantum chips needed in massive quantities for Promethium science

### Design Principles
- Hub + rocket launcher + power plant + bus build + ice platforms + fluoroketone recycling + fuel
- Legendary cryogenic science production integrated into city blocks
- 128,000 science per pickup batch from Aquilo
- Constrain production to maintain even distribution across all launchers

### Ratios & Numbers
- 94 cryogenic science/sec per cryogenic plant
- 64,000 science per quick-launch batch (two rapid launches of 32,000)
- 128,000 science per full idle pickup
- 24 fusion power cells per full spaceship round trip

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not keep old substation grid layout; convert to city blocks for scalability
- Quantum chip production planning must happen alongside cryogenic science
- Do not let all launchers fire simultaneously; stagger for even output
