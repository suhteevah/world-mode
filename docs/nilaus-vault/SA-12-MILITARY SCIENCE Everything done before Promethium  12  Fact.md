## Summary
### Key Lessons
- Military science is the last science before Promethium; built on Vulcanus
- Cannot be made on spaceships due to massive stone requirements for walls
- Uses same lava-to-stone technique as purple science (nuclear reactor approach)
- Split belt feeding into quadrants for even distribution across assemblers

### Space Age Mechanics
- Military science on Vulcanus leverages lava for stone bricks (same as purple science)
- Coal and stone are the dominant material requirements
- Iron and steel requirements are modest compared to stone/coal

### Design Principles
- 240 military science/sec per blade/module; need 4 for 960/sec total
- Split inbound belts into halves then quarters for even assembler feeding
- Two blueprints needed: lava maker + main production build

### Ratios & Numbers
- 240 military science/sec per module; 960/sec total from 4 modules
- Research productivity level 72 completed; level 73 costs 602 million science
- Massive stone and coal consumption; modest iron/steel

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not try military science in space (stone requirements make it impossible)
- Even belt distribution is critical; use cascade splitting (half then quarter)
- Lava maker blueprint must be placed before main production blueprint
