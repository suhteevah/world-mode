## Summary
### Key Lessons
- A single oil processing block can produce all three oil byproducts: lubricant, sulfuric acid, and sulfur
- Self-balancing design: lubricant is priority; when lubricant tanks are full, excess heavy oil is cracked and redirected to sulfuric acid/sulfur
- This one block unlocks multiple future builds: blue circuits (sulfuric acid), blue science (sulfur), yellow science (lubricant), accumulators (batteries from sulfuric acid)
- The block uses 5 train stations: oil in, iron in, lubricant out, sulfuric acid out, sulfur out (plus fuel train)
- Liquid handling via trains requires careful design to ensure trains can always fully unload

### Design Principles
- Oil train unloading: must guarantee the unloading tanks are completely empty before summoning another train; use circuit conditions monitoring tank levels
- No pumps at unloading - use gravity/inserter-based unloading so trains don't get stuck with partial loads
- Self-balancing oil: heavy oil -> lubricant (priority), then crack excess heavy -> light oil; light oil -> petroleum; petroleum -> plastic/sulfur
- Sulfuric acid needs iron plates as an additional input, so the block requires an iron inbound train too
- Block fits in 3 city blocks: stackers, train stations, and production area
- Each output product gets its own dedicated train station with fluid wagons

### Ratios & Numbers
- Fluid wagon capacity: 25,000 per wagon; 8 wagons = 200,000 fluid per train
- Oil unloading buffer: 100,000 per storage tank (one train's worth)
- Block width: 3 city blocks (5 material trains + 1 fuel train)
- Products: lubricant (for belts, yellow science), sulfuric acid (for blue circuits, batteries), sulfur (for blue science, explosives)
- Iron input needed: relatively small amount for sulfuric acid production

### Mistakes to Avoid
- Do not place pumps between train fluid unloading and storage tanks - it can cause trains to get stuck partially loaded
- Do not let all three outputs back up simultaneously or the entire block stalls (ensure at least one consumer is active)
- Do not forget iron input for sulfuric acid production - it is easy to miss in an oil-focused build
- Oil train buffer tanks must be fully empty before requesting next train, or trains will arrive unable to unload and block the stacker
